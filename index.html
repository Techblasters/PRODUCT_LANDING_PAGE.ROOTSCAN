The Challenge

The Hidden Problem
in Farming

Most farmers irrigate based on guesswork, routine, or visual signs. This leads to water waste, crop stress, and lost income — all without knowing it.

💸

Water Wasted

Unnecessary irrigation drains resources and raises costs without any benefit to the crop.

🌾

Crop Stress

Delaying water when crops need it damages yield quality and quantity at harvest.

📍

Uneven Soil

Soil moisture varies across a field — one-point guesses leave patches dry or over-watered.

☁️

Weather Ignored

Daily weather changes affect water loss, but irrigation schedules stay the same.

👉 This leads to lower yield, higher costs, and avoidable water loss — every single season.
Process

How Oriva Works

Five intelligent steps — from probe to prediction — so you always know exactly what your field needs.

1

Insert the Probe

Push Oriva into your soil. It instantly measures moisture levels at multiple depths — getting the full picture, not just the surface.

Multi-depth sensingPlug & measure
2

Collect Environmental Data

Simultaneously reads surrounding conditions: air temperature, humidity, and light intensity in real time.

TemperatureHumidityLight intensity
3

Smart Calculation

Runs the scientifically validated ET₀ evapotranspiration model, adjusted for your specific crop type using the Kc coefficient.

ET₀ ModelCrop factor (Kc)
4

Prediction Engine

Calculates how fast water will be lost from the soil over the coming days — accounting for weather, crop stage, and current reserves.

Water balance trackingDepletion forecast
5

Clear Decision Output

Oriva gives you a simple, actionable result — no graphs, no guessing, no expertise needed.

✅ No irrigation needed today
⏳ Irrigate after 2 days
⚠️ Water now
